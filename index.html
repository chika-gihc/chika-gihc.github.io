✦ About me ✦
Hello!
I'm Chika. An independent VTuber & Illustrator based in Japan.
Previously, I was responsible for concept creation and character design, primarily working on Book covers and Illustrations, Mobile game character designs, and Trading card game illustrations for corporate and individual clients.
A few years ago, I experienced an illness. Currently, I focus on creating original concepts and character designs featuring Cute Female Characters, and I accept commissions from companies and individuals.
✦ Please feel free to contact me anytime! ✦

こんにちは、日本在住の 個人VStreamer & 絵描き のChikaと申します。

数年前に病気を患い、現在は自身の大好きな可愛らしい女の子キャラクターの原案・デザインを中心に、企業様・個人様を問わずお仕事を承っております。
